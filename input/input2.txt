The date of the winter solstice in the Roman Calendar marks the birth of Jesus. It is commonly celebrated on December 25 all across the globe. The festival encourages us to make merry and be happy. Its sole aim is to teach us that the light of the lord always guards us and that we must never lose hope. Jesus was born as a ray of hope to the oppressed masses. The birth of Jesus saw the end of the sufferings of plenty of people. It is the time when people leave all other work and get together to celebrate the festival with joy.
